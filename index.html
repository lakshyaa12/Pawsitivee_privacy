The Problem

Street animals often suffer from injuries, dehydration, heatstroke, and other emergencies. Most people want to help, but don’t know what to do — or worse, accidentally make the situation worse.

Our Solution

Pawsitivee provides simple, step-by-step first aid guidance that anyone can follow. It helps users take the right action quickly and safely during animal emergencies.

Key Features

• Emergency guides for common situations

• Clear Do’s & Don’ts to avoid mistakes

• Vital check tools like breathing rate

• Works offline — no internet required

How It Works

1. Select the animal

2. Choose the issue

3. Follow step-by-step guidance

4. Use vitals to assess condition
